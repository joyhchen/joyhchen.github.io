Save Money

Financial Benefits

Recycling can not only benefit the planet we live on but also your wallet. Try our interactive tools that determine just how much you can save by choosing more eco-friendly ways to live.

Buy a reusable water bottle.

While one gallon of tap water costs around $0.004, a gallon of bottled water will cost around $1.22. Every year, millions of water bottles are tossed in the trash, and by buying water bottles, you may find yourself throwing them away occasionally. By buying a tumbler or any other reusable water bottle, you can ensure that more plastic isn't generated and contribute to helping the Earth. Try out our interactive tool below to see just how much you can save by choosing to fill your reusable water bottle with tap water instead of buying disposable plastic water bottles.

Change your toilet paper.

In the following examples, we compare the cost of using Charmin Ultra Strong toilet paper with 0% recyclable content and Natural Value toilet paper with 100% recyclable content. Note that we are simply comparing the costs between the two toilet paper products and not the quality. It is for you to decide whether or not the quality of one brand is worth more than another, as that was not one of the factors we considered when writing the equation for this slider. We did not pick the brands featured either. Rather, we referenced a study and used the data provided there to create the example you will see below.

Conclusion

Hopefully, our examples have helped you see just how much money can be saved by being eco-friendly. When you recycle, you are not only helping the Earth but also keeping money in your wallet. Keep in mind that each slider only goes up to one-hundred. As you increase the number of units, the amount you save also goes up.
