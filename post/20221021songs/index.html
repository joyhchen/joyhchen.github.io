Playlist: week of 10-21-2022

Oct 19, 2022

Bringing back 2000s-era blogging, where I talk about anything and everything that interests me on my blog. I am particularly interested in music none of my friends listen to. As a result, I talk about it on my blog, into the void!

Don’t Break My Heart

One of my favorite TV/film cliches is small town to big city. This song plays in the background when the main character in Lou Ye’s Summer Palace leaves for Beijing.

向前走

This song also fits into the small town to big city cliche. I especially like the hopefulness in lines like “不管如何路是自己走” because it’s inspiring. My parents have a family friend from Taiwan. He’s now in his sixties, but this is still his favorite song. This song inspired a whole generation, and it continues to do so.

One Night in Beijing

Midnight tunes in Sanlitun.

아! 옛날이여

I used to be a huge fan of the Korean variety show Immortal Songs. After Lee Sunhee appeared in an episode, I listened to half her discography and found myself liking this song the most.

15の夜

Another category of songs I found myself drawn to can be succinctly described as “not wanting to do what other people want you to do,” and I would say this one is among my favorites in that category.

闷

A song that sits well in the category defined above.

自由

I first heard a cover of this song in 2013. I much prefer the original version!

챠우챠우

It sounds like nostalgia.
