Album: 新裤子 New Pants - Go East

Nov 7, 2022

Go East

“Go East” was released in 2009, a year after Beijing hosted the 2008 Summer Olympics. The title is a reference to the song “Go West” (sidenote: featured in my favorite movie Mountains May Depart).

The dialogue throughout the song is sampled from the film 《不是为了爱情》 (Not for Love). For research purposes, I decided to watch the clip the song sampled from.

“但是我不能为这离开我的祖国！”

I can’t leave my motherland for this!

“你可以去国外，世界那么大，你会找到爱人的！”

You can go abroad. The world is so big. You’ll find someone to love!

“我不去，我是中国人呐！”

I won’t go. I’m Chinese!

我爱你

我不想失去你

This album is iconic! I’m unable to find any English-language media about it on the internet, so here’s my contribution.
