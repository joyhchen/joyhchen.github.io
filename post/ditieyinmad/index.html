Aug 13, 2022

What is Bilibili?

Bilibili is a video-sharing website known for bullet comments, live comments that play over videos in real time (more: What is Bilibili?).

What is 音MAD?

音MAD (pronounced: yin mad, literally “sound MAD”) is a category of video where a song and some audio source are remixed together. 地铁音MAD (pronounced: di tie yin mad, literally: “subway sound MAD”) is a subset of 音MAD videos that remixes the sounds of public transportation with popular songs for an interesting result. You can find hundreds of these videos on Bilibili (!), and a lot of them have English “subtitles” because people like to leave bullet comments that are the English translation of whatever is being said in the video (for fun).

Ordinary Modu Subway Disco

One of the most famous 地铁音MAD videos is “普通的魔都地铁Disco” (“Ordinary Modu Subway Disco”). The “modu” in the video title comes from Shanghai’s nickname (see: Why is Shanghai called Modu?).

“Ordinary Disco” is a popular Chinese song originally sung by Chinese vocaloids. Since its release, it has been covered by mainstream pop artists Li Yuchun and Wang Feng (my favorite version).

Fantastic Metro: Shenzhen Metro vs. Beijing Subway

Two subway systems x “Fantastic Baby” (The 2012 BIGBANG hit, one of the most well-known K-pop songs of all time). The result: “Fantastic Metro”.
