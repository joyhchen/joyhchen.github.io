Organize a Drive

A recycling drive and attract an entire community and educate thousands of people about the benefits of recycling through promotion. As a school or business, a recycling drive would be similar to a toy drive.
The general idea is to advertise a day where anyone can drop off their recyclable items for transportation to a recycling plant. This event would require contact with companies willing to help you drive recyclable items to their correct location and volunteers who would like to help out for a few hours to an entire day.
Recycling drives can be a great community event, and they would educate more people through advertising. Some people who may not even have an interest in donating may be further educated about recycling. If you are interested in organizing a recycling drive, please see the contact page and e-mail our team for more information.
