My favorite songs in Mandarin

May 17, 2023

I’m a firm believer that most people’s favorite songs were simply there for them at the right place and right time in their lives. I’m no different. At this point, I think my list of all-time favorite Mandopop songs is almost set in stone.

Don’t Break my Heart #

I like that it’s an upbeat rock song. I first heard it in a movie about Beijing while living in Beijing (and Dou Wei is from Beijing). All in all, it reminds me a lot of Beijing.

童年 #

I like singing this at karaoke as an adult because it reminds me of how simple things were when I was a kid, and it’s like, there’s no reason my life can’t be that simple now. That was also back when we had blackboards. Now they’re all dry erase.

爱人错过 #

They have the most interesting new music.

我是不是你最疼爱的人 #

I found the emotion in this song easy to understand. Xuan Xuan’s cover will always be the best version to me.

夜空中最亮的心 #

You know that one Chinese song that sounds like Coldplay but isn’t. I’ve replayed it a million times though.

也罢 #

I like how she sings with emotion so effortlessly, and it feels genuine, not overdone.

静止 #

All around extremely underrated.

思念是一种病 #

I first heard Robynn & Kendy’s cover of this song on the third season of The Voice of China. When they transitioned to “Where is the Love” I thought it was one of the best auditions I’d ever seen on the show. I love the original too. It’s a classic.

无常 #

Fuzao is Faye Wong’s best album.

千里之外 #

My dad would play Still Fantasy all the time in the car growing up. It remains my favorite Jay Chou album to this day.

春天里 #

I like all of Wang Feng’s popular songs. They sound nice.

太阳 #

No, not that one. Not her other song, or the other one.

无与伦比的美丽 #

。

大艺术家 #

It’s a really good pop song.

日常 #

I like songs about living life. “To Hebe” is good in that regard too.

假装我们没爱过 #

I feel like this song was lost to time. I also like that one Fahrenheit song with the MV set in the desert (?). That one is more famous.

牡丹江 #

You know when you listen to a song about a place, and even though you’ve never been there, you can feel how deeply the person who wrote the song feels about that place.
