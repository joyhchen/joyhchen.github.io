Make a Difference

More than 50% of materials thrown in the trash could be recycled. As our landfills grow bigger by the year, it becomes increasingly important for us to recognize the importance of recycling and the positive effects it brings to our environment.

Our
Mission

Did you know millions of plastic water bottles end up in landfills when they could be recycled? We hope to create a cleaner, more liveable Earth by educating others about recycling and its benefits.

Our Impact

The amount of waste in the world is negatively impacting the state of our planet. Recycling a small piece of paper or can of soda can make a huge difference.

By the Numbers
