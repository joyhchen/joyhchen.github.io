About

Every day, truckloads of trash are thrown away to rot in our planet's landfills, and in those familiar black plastic bags lie thousands of items that could be recycled. Whether it be a sheet of paper or an aluminum can, every item makes a difference, and every item can contribute to the recreation of essential everyday items. We hope you take the time to consider the cause we are trying to promote and consider the effects of trash on the world we live in. If you would like more personalized information, feel free to e-mail our team at info@recycling-illinois.com. We are constantly trying to improve the information on our website and educate as many people as we can about recycling.

Introduce Change

While not everything can be recycled, many items that could get tossed in the wrong can. You can read our facts pages to learn more about a growing problem that can be solved by simply spreading the word. Every share on social media can lead to more informed individuals, so we encourage you to introduce change to your community and circle of friends!
