Donate Items and Time

If you don't have any spare change to donate, no problem! You can recycle by donating your spare items like coats, gloves, shirts, and sweatshirts that have outgrown you or a family member. Organizations like the Salvation Army set up donation centers just for people like you who want to make sure we waste less and share more.

If you don't have any items you need to donate, consider donating your time to educational classes about recycling. All it takes is offering your time to a school, business, or community center. If you aren't comfortable teaching classes, you can also try volunteering. It doesn't have to be a regular occurence. One hour of volunteering is enough to make a difference.
